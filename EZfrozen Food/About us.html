    Our History





    EZfrozen Food, started from the home business in 2019 whereby we produce the frozen "kuih-muih" products at first such as curry puff, steam bun, doughnuts and market it through the social media. In 2021, the beginning of the year which is 7th January 2021, EZfrozen Food successfully achieved first achievement which is bought for the own property at Jalan Sutera, Johor Bahru to run the frozen food business.

    EZfrozen Food also started improve and produce the variation of food products that some of them are acquired from the suppliers and handmade. For improving the business was really takes time but EZfrozen Food will never give up to achieve our mission and vision of the company's establishment.
